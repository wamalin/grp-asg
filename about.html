Our Doctors

1. Dr. Amalin

Full Name Dr. Wan Nurul Amalin Binti Wan Asik
Position Founder and Managing Director
Experience Dr. Wan Nurul Amalin is the founder and medical director of Aesthetica, which is located in Singapore. In 2005, she graduated with honours from the Manipal Academy of Higher Education in India with a Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) degree. She continued her education and received a postgraduate Diploma in Dermatology from the prestigious Cardiff University in the United Kingdom in 2009, followed by a Diploma in Aesthetic Medicine from the American Academy of Aesthetic Medicine in the United States in 2010.


2. Dr. Iwana

Full Name Dr. Nur Iwana Binti Azman
Position Aesthetic Doctor
Experience Dr. Nur Iwana received her medical degree from the Asian Institute of Medicine, Science, and Technology (AIMST). As a result, Dr. Iwana went on to complete his postgraduate training in medical aesthetics at the Medical Aesthetic Certification Programme in Malaysia.


3. Dr. Zulnajwa

Full Name Dr. Siti Zulnajwa Natasha Binti Azri
Position Aesthetic Doctor
Experience Dr. Zulnajwa Natasha received her medical degree from the University of Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS) (MD). She then pursued a post-graduate certification from the American Academy of Aesthetic Medicine (AAAM) and the Medical Aesthetic Certification (MAC) Program due to her interest in aesthetic medicine. She is now a licensed medical aesthetician.
