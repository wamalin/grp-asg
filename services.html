Services Details

1. Face

  • Active Acne and Scarring

    Acne is one of the most frequent skin disorders among teens and young adults, if not the most common. It is a chronic inflammatory illness of our skin's hair follicles and oil glands. Although acne is most typically found on the face, it can also be found in locations with a high concentration of oil glands, such as the chest and back. Acne, if left untreated, can cause lifelong damage and scarring, lowering a person's quality of life.

    Acne treatment focuses on addressing the source of the acne — reducing oil and germs. Acne is best managed by a multimodal strategy that involves drugs, peels, facials, and numerous innovative light and laser treatments, among other things.

    Anyone who has suffered from acne will tell you that scarring is one of the most unpleasant aspects of the condition. Acne scars are classified into two types: post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation scars and deep acne scars. Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation is characterized by a transitory change in skin colour at the site of injury or inflammation, in this example, acne. Hyperpigmented patches are often red, brown, or black. This skin darkening is transient and will diminish with time.
  • Facial Contouring

    Facial contouring and shaping without surgery are becoming increasingly fashionable. At aesthetica.my, we use the most recent technology and materials to help you obtain a natural and desired appearance. Every layer of the face, from the skin to the bone, is affected by the ageing process. Understanding this principle allows us to employ a variety of approaches to combat the natural process of ageing.
  • Skin Tightening and Lifting

    Sagging skin is one of the most unappealing indicators of ageing. Our skin loses collagen and elastin as we age, both of which are important in preserving the skin's integrity. However, as we age, not just our skin but also the internal support beneath the skin suffers. Treating both the skin and the internal support is essential for producing a natural, long-lasting, and appealing appearance.

2. Skin

  • Pigmentation and Skin Lightening

    Everyone desires beautiful and vibrant skin. One of the most prevalent types of treatments we see in our office is for skin colouring and blemishes. It is critical to recognise that there are several forms of skin pigmentation. Some pigmentation, such as freckles and age spots, is readily erased, however others, such as melasma, hyperpigmentation, and other forms of birthmarks, need a great deal more attention and care.
  • Hydration

    It is critical to maintain our skin healthy and moisturised since it performs several vital dynamic activities in safeguarding our bodies. Skin moisture and oil production decrease dramatically as we age, thus it is critical to maintain the skin moist. Skin that is moisturised and radiant is a sign of youth.
  • Wrinkles/Pores/Skin Texture

    Have you ever pondered why some people appear considerably younger than their age, while others appear much older? Because the skin is the biggest organ in the body, environmental variables have the most impact on it. Wrinkling, rough textures, pigmentation, and sagginess are all signs of ageing skin. Even if we cannot stop the ageing process completely, there are techniques to decrease the indications and restore a youthful natural appearance.

    When it comes to weight loss and body sculpting, it's always crucial to maintain a healthy lifestyle. This involves a healthy diet as well as frequent exercise.   • Stretch Marks

    In Stretch Marks Treatment, we use Fractional CO2 Laser, Rejuran Healer and PRP (Platelet Rich Plasma).

    Fractional Laser Resurfacing is a novel method that promises outcomes comparable to deep chemical peels or ablative laser resurfacing without the agony and inconvenience of a protracted healing time.

    Rejuran Healer is a skin booster injectable that promotes wound healing while also having an anti-inflammatory impact on your body. Rejuran Healer can enhance skin elasticity, texture, and tone, as well as decrease wrinkles and fine lines, increase thickness, and hydrate the skin.

    PRP is an abbreviation for platelet rich plasma. Blood is extracted from the patient's body and deposited in a machine that separates blood cells, plasma, and platelets. PRP is made by collecting platelets and plasma and eliminating any leftover blood cells, thus the name platelet-rich plasma. It is also referred to as 'Vampire' treatment.
