Top 5 Award Winning Procedures

1. Ultheraphy

Ultherapy uses time-tested micro-focused ultrasound energy to lift and tighten the skin on the face and neck without surgery or downtime.

2. PicoSure Laser

Picosure’s PressureWave TM technology uses a unique wavelength of light (755nm) to treat wrinkles, acne scars, and blemishes.

3. Dual Yellow Laser

The gentle yet advanced Dual Yellow Laser utilizes yellow and green light to treat a wide range of skin conditions with little to no discomfort.

4. Rejuran Healer

Rejuran is an anti-aging injection treatment derived from salmon DNA, that regenerates and hydrates all the layers of the skin.

5. Silk Peel

Silk Peel is the non-invasive dermatological treatment that simultaneously exfoliates with condition serums for an all-inclusive treatment.
